Operation: Memory Game

How many pairs of pictures?

How many seconds?


This game will test your memory.

To begin, choose how many pairs of pictures you would like to memorize. Next, choose how many seconds you would like to see the images before they flip back over. The shorter the time, the harder the game. You will have a limited amount of time to match all the images.

Click "Start" when you are ready to begin. You may also begin a new game at any time by clicking the "New game" button.
